Capital Ale House has to be my favorite place to eat in Harrisonburg. The food is great, my favorite is the margherita pizza. In addition to the food, the beer on draft is great.

I love coming here with both friends and family. When I graduate in the spring, this is something that I will miss most!

Author's Rating: 10/10

Capital Ale House is a great place with great food AND beer. I personally love a lot of the menu. One downside are the prices as it is a fancier restaurant, but I usually can convince my parents to take me when they're here, so I still give it a 10.
