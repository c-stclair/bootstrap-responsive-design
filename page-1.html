Billy Jacks, home of the infamous "sticky nuggs." Prior to eating here for the first time four years ago, I had my doubts about the menu, as some of the names are a little odd, but nevertheless the food is amazing.

Whether you'd like a burger, wings, or even a deep fried oreo, Billy Jacks has it all! Definitely a staple of downtown Harrisonburg, but make sure to get there early on gameday as the restaurant fills up quickly!

Author's Rating: 9/10

Billy Jacks is a solid choice for anyone visiting Harrisonburg, the food is great and the atmosphere sets the bar high. The only downside is the long wait times because of the smaller restaurant. Overall, a great pick though!
