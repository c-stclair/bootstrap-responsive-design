Top Places to Eat in Harrisonburg

From the perspective of a JMU student

Harrisonburg Virginia, home of the Dukes, offers so much to both year-round residents and students alike. Nestled in the Shenandoah Valley, Harrisonburg has great options for all types of visitors. Places to eat, mountains to hike, wineries to visit, and much, much more.

While Harrisonburg is a college town, the restaurants downtown are home for those that live here permanently. The blend of townies and students, truly make downtown Harrisonburg a site to see.

As a student here at James Madison University, I've had the opportunity to experience a lot of what Harrisonburg has to offer, but not everything of course. Going to school during a pandemic doesn't exactly leave room for new experiences.

Regardless, I decided to cover my top places to eat here in Harrisonburg. Feel free to check them out for yourself and let me know how your experience goes!
